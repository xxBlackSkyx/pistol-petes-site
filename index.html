Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mobile detailing services.

Are you mobile?
Yes — we are fully mobile, servicing Palm Coast, Ormond Beach, Hammock, Daytona Beach, St. Augustine, and Flagler Beach. We bring our own power and water to every appointment. No shop visit required.
What is a ceramic coating?
A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer containing silicon dioxide (SiO₂) that chemically bonds to your vehicle's paint, creating a durable, hydrophobic layer. It protects against UV rays, dirt, bird droppings, and contaminants while making your vehicle easier to clean and enhancing gloss.
What are your prices?
Stage 1 In/Out is $150 flat for all vehicle sizes. Stage 2 ranges from $200 (coupes) to $275 (trucks/3-row SUVs). Stage 3 ranges from $300 to $375. XL lifted trucks and vans may vary. We quote based on vehicle size and condition.
How long does a detail take?
Anywhere from 1 to 10+ hours depending on the service and vehicle condition. Stage 1 is typically 1-2 hours, Stage 2 around 3-5 hours, and Stage 3 can take 6-10+ hours for the full premium treatment.
How do I schedule?
Text us at (386) 393-1772 or fill out the contact form below. Pete personally handles every booking to ensure the right package and availability for your vehicle.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Palm Coast, Ormond Beach, Hammock, Daytona Beach, St. Augustine, Flagler Beach, and surrounding areas across Flagler and Volusia counties.
